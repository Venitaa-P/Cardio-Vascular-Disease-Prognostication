Our Mission

Our mission is to develop an intelligent, reliable, and accessible system for the early prediction of heart disease. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and clinically relevant data, we aim to assist individuals and healthcare providers in identifying potential heart conditions before they become critical. We are committed to enhancing awareness, enabling proactive health decisions, and reducing the risks associated with late diagnosis.
